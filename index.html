About City School

Watch the video to learn more about The City School

Established in Karachi in 1978, The City School is an institution of excellence where the highest standards are maintained in order to impart quality education to its pupils. Since its inception, the school has seen tremendous growth and success. It now constitutes one of the largest and most progressive chains of school of South Asia. 178 school in 51 cities across 4 countries More than 60,000 students Over 8,000 employees Overseas location (U.A.E, Saudi Arabia, Bangladesh, Malaysia, and Philippines) The City School continues to expand its reach to meet the demands for quality education. The knowledge and skills-based curriculum derived from the UK national curriculum guides students from Nursery to Cambridge International Examinations (for the 16 and 18 year age group), qualifying them for the IGCSE, and O and A Level examinations. The uniform implementation of the curriculum gives parents, students, and teachers the advantage of a smooth and efficient transfer from one school to another within the system.
